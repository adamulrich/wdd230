Demographics

With 75,000 residents, Briar Glen has a vibrant and growing community. 40% of our community was born outside the US, thus we embrace many cultures. We are also highly educated, with 96% of adults having a HS Diploma, and 71% of adults over 25 having a bachelors degree.

Location

Nestled in the cascade foothills east of Seattle by 15 miles, Briar Glen is modern, yet rural. Year round and seasonal activities are available. From sailing on Lake Samammish or Lake Washington or the Puget Sound, to hiking, fishing, and camping, to skiing and snowboarding, we have it all.

Desirability

Briar Glen is a premier destination because of it's numerous Fortune 500 companies, primarily in the software and bio-tech sectors. Because of the diverse nature of the people that are attracted to our city, we have wonderful urban spaces as well as many traditional residential areas.

Retail

Briar Glen boasts the largest outdoor retail space on the Eastside. With beautiful outdoor spaces, we balance enjoying the Great Pacific NW outdoors with excellent retailing. In addition, Downtown Briar Glen has over 500 local businesses, many of whom are Chamber members.

Local Government

The City of Briar Glen has the lowest local tax rate and B&O tax rate, of any City in King County. This encourages businesses to want to be here, and helps keep our economy humming.
