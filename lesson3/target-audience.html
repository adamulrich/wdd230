Target Audience

Businesses of all kinds, including:

  • Retailers
  • Craftsmen
  • Traders
  • Merchants
  • Non-Profits
  • Service providers, such as legal, financial, marketing, sales and insurance
  • Small businesses, including home-based businesses

Scenarios
